THE STORY BEHIND

How Phantoms were before passing away

FIRSTLY! WHO WERE THE GUYZ?

They were simple, as us, guyz whose like to enjoy their lives doing simple things, but somehow, things happened..


Illustrations about "The Guyz":
- Before passing away, our Phantoms were knew as The Simple Guyz, which will be our next project, focused in our Phantoms while they were alive. You can check some artwoks about the guyz in our gallery.

Working on Phantoms:
- Everyday our team gets reunited and discuss about our whole project in order to bring you guys the best we can do. Our Artwork is constantly being improved, and we will always maintain that aspect of evolution.

Discord, WebPage and Twitter promotion:
- We are trying to reach the most people we can during the Q1 2022. That's when we start growing our community, webpage, Discord server and Twitter.

PHANTOMS FIRST SEASON AFTERLAUNCH

What's next?


Art Improvement:
We are always improving our artwork, studying new possibilities to do a better job and improve our project in general, we've always done that and want to keep doing it.

Starting to work on Guyz Launch:
Our next project will be the Simple Guyz, which will be based in our Phantoms while they were alive. After Phantoms launch, we will start to work on the guyz while working on the Phantoms S2.

Working on Phantoms S2:
As said before, we want to release a S2 of our lovely Phantoms, after S1, we'll work on that as well as working on Simple Guyz.

Studying fusing system:
One of our goals is to bring a fusing system to ADA Phantoms. It will demand certain time, but we'll start to study and work on manners to bring that to our project.

Giveaways, Charities and Community:
After S1 launch, we want to bring giveaways to our community, as well as donations to charities. We'll still work on growing our community and we want you guys to stay tuned on all our next steps after S1 launch.

SIMPLE GUYZ..? WHAT IT MEANS?

Cool art, cool concept, confuse stories but simple guyz.


Releasing and promoting Simple Guyz:
After Phantoms S1, we'll start to work on Simple Guyz, developing its artwork, story, socialmedias and webpage.

Working truly hard on improvement:
It's really important for us to bring you guys the best we can. With that in mind, we will always keep improving not only the Phantoms, but also Simple Guyz and any other future projects we have in mind.

Special editions and collabs:
We like the idea of special editions, and as well as in the Phantoms, we also want to bring some special Guyz, which can be easily recognized as different if compared to other ones. We want to bring collabs with other projects, and grow our community and project.

Simple Guyz launch:
After everything is done and our artwork gets finished, we want to launch Simple Guyz. Our goal is to launch it on Q2 2022.

PHANTOMS SEASON 2

Season two of the cutest phantoms living on the chain


Finishing Fusing API:
One of our goals is to bring the possibility to holders to fuse their phantoms and create a new one based on the characteristics of the Phantoms used.

Putting creativity to work:
We are always trying to create new kinds of assets and we aim to keep doing that, exerciting our creativity in order to bring you guys the best we can.

Community participation:
We want you guys to participate in all the process of Phantoms developing, after S1, we'll try to collect more information from our community, like which kinds of assets they'd like to see for example, in order to bring everyone an amazing S2.

Phantoms S2 launch:
After all the steps and all the needed improvements, S2 will come to haunt everyone.
